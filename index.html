BIO

Having been working in the UX/UI space for the last 15 years, Pete has had a broad range of experience working at all levels of leadership and on many varied projects.

Since the beginning of his career, Pete has been working with mobile companies and services, back when Nokia ruled the world and the iPhone was not yet a glint in Apple's eye.

Having 'been around a hile' Pete has worked on many mobile projects, web sites, web apps and installed desktop software. He is no stranger to rolling up his sleeves and getting involved with day to day deliverables, as well as taking a step back to lead, inspire and plan.

This portfolio is a small taste of what he has acheived over the years, and hopefully it will inspire you to want to learn more!
